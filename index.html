My Heart Behind This

Rooted, refined, and re-rooted in joy.

As a homeschool mom of four, I'm passionate about nurturing both learning and family life in a way that feels joyful and meaningful.

Authentic Joy in the Journey and Bloom and Blossom were born from my own story — a season of motherhood that was uprooted, refined, and ultimately re-rooted in joy. It wasn't in the perfection, but in the becoming, that purpose began to take root.

From that place, these spaces were created — a calling to walk alongside other mothers learning to grow, bloom, and find joy right where they are. My hope is that you leave feeling seen, strengthened, and reminded that even here, even now… you can bloom.

— Christina
